490 Military Occupational Specialties

Every soldier is assigned an MOS - a job code defining their military role. The Army encompasses everything from infantry and armor to cybersecurity, healthcare, and logistics.

490Active Army MOS codes

25U - Signal Operations Support

Signal soldiers maintain Army communications networks. Their civilian equivalents - network administrators, computer support specialists, IT analysts - are in high demand.

$76,827avg civilian salary for 25U

Direct matches and related paths

Dark bars are direct MOS-to-civilian crosswalk matches (DoD MILX). Lighter bars are related occupations in the same field - expanding the opportunity set beyond the obvious career paths.

BLS OEWS May 2024 state-level estimates

18.3 million veterans across 3,150 counties

After service, veterans settle across the country - but not randomly. Sun Belt states, areas near major military bases, and coastal metros draw disproportionate shares.

18.3MU.S. veterans (VetPop 2023)

Maricopa, Los Angeles, San Diego lead all counties

Maricopa County, AZ - home to Luke AFB and a major VA hub - leads with 246,000 veterans. California, Texas, and Florida dominate the top of the list.

246Kveterans in Maricopa County, AZ
